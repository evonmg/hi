Piano Trio (2023)

This was my final for Intro Composition. I wrote it in like 4 days over Thanksgiving break. I tried experimenting with register and harmony to create this interesting gloom effect that serves as the undercurrent for the entire piece, and wanted to see how hard I could push my own sense of tonality to create new and interesting colors.

pain and suffering (2023)

This was my spring semester Fermata 24 composition! Every semester the Fermata Composers Collective at Brown holds an event where composers write a piece from 7pm-7am, and performers rehearse it until it gets performed at a concert at 7pm the next day (hence, 24). I wrote this in about 5 hours because they told me I was writing for solo trumpet and I had no idea what I was supposed to do so I came up with this monstrosity. Featuring impossible trumpet runs, variations on "Never Gonna Give You Up", "Cbat", and ending with a fun surprise (the lick)!! Enjoy!!!
